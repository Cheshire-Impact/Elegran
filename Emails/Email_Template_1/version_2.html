Manhattan Sales Prices Reach Record Low Levels in Q3 2019
 

Manhattan’s residential market experienced a steep drop in sales prices and volume during Q3, a significant change from the high sales recorded in the previous quarter. These results came after a surge in sales during the second quarter as buyers rushed to close deals before NYC’s mansion tax was implemented on July 1. The current state of the market is giving homebuyers the advantage, as this means higher inventory and better deals throughout the city.
